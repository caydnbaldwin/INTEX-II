How the feedback loop works: When you mark a donor as Converted ✓ or Declined ✗ using the outcome buttons, that signal is saved to feedback_log.json. The next time the pipeline runs, it reads those labels and uses them to retrain the model — so donors who actually upgrade become positive training examples. Over time, this makes the upgrade_probability scores more accurate than the rule-based labels alone.
